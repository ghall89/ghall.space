# AI Policy

While I believe AI has legitimate applications, it has many functional, environmental, societal, and economic drawbacks that need to be addressed. That being said, even in a perfect world where all those issues are addressed, generative AI has _no place_ in the realm of human expression.

I firmly believe that if someone couldn't be bothered to write something, then nobody should be bothered to read it. As such, it is my policy that **none** of the content on this site is produced with generative AI. 

However, AI _is_ used during the editing process to catch spelling and grammatical errors. It's never used to change or add any content, and all edits are done manually to avoid blindly copy-pasting. 

The exception to this policy is the extremely rare case where the generated content is directly in service of the human-written content, such as illustrative examples, which will be clearly marked on the page, or called out in the body of the text.

Here is a current list of pages and blog posts that contain generative AI content:

- [Creating a Dating Profile With AI](/blog/2022/creating-a-dating-profile-with-ai) - A single generated image, and clearly marked quotations from generated output.

Additionally, I will make no effort to make any content on this site "AI friendly", or provide any sort of optimizations for AI search. I write for human beings, and I do not consent to anything I've written to be summarized or otherwise altered by agentic browsers, or any other AI tools. 

Furthermore, I reserve the right to actively hinder the use of AI agents.
